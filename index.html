Many business owners do underestimate the importance of music in their businesses and its effect on all people around. For example, it makes employees more productive, especially younger generation. It is no news that music has a unique capacity to put people in different emotional states. Many researches have shown that background music in commercial environments affects customers to stay longer and buy more.

Background music can be used as a tool to arouse the right associations and create a retail environment that reflects the brand identity. It has also been shown to reduce the perceived waiting time and add to the impression that the company cares about its customers.

What is Songo?

Songo is customisable music for your business, played automatically with the help of an algorithm, following the music preferences of your customers and employees.

Songoroo is neither a passive in-store radio nor a jukebox. We developed a platform which combines the best from both concepts, presenting the customers with the opportunity to influence music playlist, but at the same time providing the business owners with variety of tools to set appropriate rules and boundaries to what kind of music can be played in their businesses,. Taking the customers’ preferences into the consideration in a fully automated fashion is exactly what differentiates Songo from other in-store radio providers.

What is our offer to you?

Interactive playlist

Music playlist in your individual locations adjusts itself continuously based on music preferences of the present Songo app users.

Business intelligence

Do you want to know what kind of customers are visiting your locations or what kind of music might increase the productivity of your employees? Our tailored reports and big data analytics will give you the overview.

Music database

With Songoroo, you will get access to the music database which can fulfil every musical wish your customers and employees might have. We will expand our database based on your specific regional wishes as well.

Customer support

With the help of our automated 24/7 monitoring system, a team of highly dedicated customer service representatives help you with any enquires that may arise.

Self managed targeted messages

Our team is ready to help you to develop and create tailored audio and banner based messages to support your sales and marketing activities but also general public announcements in-between the played songs.

Admin dashboard

One-stop-solution combining all tools to manage your music, playlists, advertisements and payments should you choose so. We can of course manage all that for you.

Mobile application

Various features in the Songo app allows your customers and employees to influence music played in your locations.

Royalty payments

We take care of all aspects of dealing with the payments and administration around royalties. This ensures that only legal music is played and allows you to concentrate on your business.
